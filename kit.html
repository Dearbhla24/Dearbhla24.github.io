First aid kit contents

A first aid kit should contain:

  • Wound dressings
  • roller bandages
  • triangular bandages
  • tubular bandages
  • disposable gloves
  • ppe
  • cleaning wipes
  • gauze
  • scissors
  • adhesive tape
  • pins and clips
  • scissors
  • saline solution
