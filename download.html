How to download the app for IOS!

  1. Go to the main page
  2. In the search bar area, click the share button (a square with an upwards arrow)
  3. Next , Scroll down until you find add to home screen. Click this!
  4. In the top right corner, you should see a button saying "add", press on this.
  5. That's it!

How to download the app for android!

  1. Go to the main page
  2. In the top right corner, click the 3 dots to open the menu
  3. Next, scroll down until you find install app/add to homescreen. Tap this!
  4. That's it!
